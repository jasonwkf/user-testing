Frequently Asked Questions

Does this package come with a print copy delivered to my home?

No, this is a digital subscription to The Straits Times and it does not include the print newspaper.

How do I know if I’m eligible for this promotion?

Yes.

How do I know if I am eligible for these subscription plans?

All corporate subscribers with a registered commercial address are eligible to subscribe.

Individual subscribers with a residential address can browse our subscription packages at www.subscribe.sph.com.sg.

When will the billing for my subscription start?

Your subscription will officially start within 1 day from the date of your purchase.

Your first billing will begin 2-3 working days after your purchase and will cover the first period of your subscription (be it monthly recurring or yearly payments) from your official subscription start date.
